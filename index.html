Lost Woods - Adelaide Local Band

The Band

Lost Woods is an Australian indie rock/pop band from Adelaide, SA. Their soaring vocals and jangly guitars have earned them many plaudits and loyal fan base. The band shared stage with some wellknown artists which include Timberwolf, Kingswood and many more. Bridget Hustwaite from Triple J applauded the band, describing them as "Punchy, jangly, soaring indie pop goodness" The bands sophomore EP 'Shaping Distant Memories' is out now. A beautiful textured and heartbreakingly melancholy record, that will have you lost in the layers of vocals and guitars.
